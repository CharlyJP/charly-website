How it works

Practice English anywhere,
any time.

Students open the Charly app and start a real conversation. No scripts, no multiple choice, just natural spoken English with an AI that listens, responds, and gently improves their language along the way.

01

Teachers set the focus

Before any call, teachers assign conversation topics and grammar focus areas for each student. Past tense, conditionals, vocabulary sets. Charly tailors every conversation to match.

02

Students call Charly

Open the app, tap call. Within seconds students are in conversation with a warm, patient English speaker who adapts to their level and keeps things natural.

03

Mistakes are corrected naturally

When a student makes a grammar error, Charly does not interrupt or correct bluntly. It weaves the correct form naturally into its next response so students hear and absorb the right pattern.

04

Feedback lands in the portal

After every call, a full transcript and AI-generated feedback report appears in the teacher portal. Grammar patterns, vocabulary gaps, and improvement over time.

For teachers

Everything you need to
track real progress.

Charly does not just give students practice. It gives teachers insight. Every conversation is logged, transcribed, and analysed so you always know exactly where each student stands.

Full call transcripts

Every word of every conversation, timestamped and searchable. See exactly what was said, how the student responded, and where they hesitated.

Grammar and vocabulary analysis

Automated feedback identifies recurring grammar issues, vocabulary gaps, and patterns, without you having to listen to a single recording.

Personalised per student

Set grammar focus and conversation topics for each student individually. Charly remembers what was discussed before and builds on it naturally over time.

Practice time tracking

Monitor how often each student is practising, set weekly call limits, and see usage trends across your whole class at a glance.

Student memory

Charly builds a picture of each student across calls. Their interests, their habits, their progress. Every conversation feels personal and continuous.

Class-wide overview

See all your students in one dashboard. Who is active, who has not practised this week, and who needs extra attention on specific skills.
