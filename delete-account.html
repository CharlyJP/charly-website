Delete your account

This page explains how to delete your Charly account and the data associated with it. Charly is provided by Charly AI Pty Ltd.

Delete your account from the app

The quickest way to delete your account is from within the Charly app:

  1. Open the Charly app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

Your account is deactivated immediately, and your personal data is permanently deleted within 14 days.

Prefer to request it by email?

If you can't access the app, you can request account and data deletion by emailing us at help@callcharly.io from the email address associated with your account. We will action deletion requests within 14 days.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your account details (name and email address)
  • Your voice recordings
  • Your conversation transcripts
  • Your feedback and progress data
  • Your usage and call history

All of the above is permanently deleted within 14 days of your request. We may retain anonymised, aggregated data that does not identify you, and we may retain limited records where required to meet a legal obligation. For more detail, see our Privacy Policy.
